Design Principles

Vivian Armstrong

Contrast

JetBlue

Here we can see how contrast works on a website. Jetblue does a wonderful job of using colors to make certain elements on their page stand out. A person's eye goes directly to the flight search area. They also use a bright orange to contrast the background to click on when ready to search.

White Space

Eden's Garden

Eden's Garden is an online company that sells essential oils. The use of white space on their website is to show how clean and soothing their products are. The white space does not overwhelm the user.

Visual Hierarchy

Target

Target uses hierarchy on their website. It helps to emphasize the products they want you see in an appealing way. Even the amount of background color on the page help to emphasize specific things on the webpage.

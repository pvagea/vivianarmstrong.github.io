Target Audience

The audience this website will cater to are parents, teachers, coaches, and students. This site will help them know what the weather is like and if a hat and sunscreen will be necessary to put on their children or if they need to provide rain gear.

Persona 1

Persona 1 is a mother who is concerned about the weather because she wants to make sure her children are dressed accordingly to go to school, to play outside, or to attend their practices.

Persona 2

Persona 2 is a soccer coach for the local league. He wants to make sure the weather is good for the days he has practices and give his players enough notice of cancellation due to the weather.

Scenarios

  • Do I need to drive my child to school because it will rain?
  • Do I need to wear sunscreen while I am at the park playing?
  • Will it be warm enough to dress my child in shorts today?
  • Do I need to bring a hat to school?
  • Do I need to cancel practice because of the weather?

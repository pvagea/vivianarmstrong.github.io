Our Adventures

Lower Salmon 5 Day Rafting

This trip begins and ends in Lewiston, Idaho for a total of 89 miles. It is a four night, five day trip. The rapids we will experience on this trip range from class II to III. This trip is perfect for families with children ages 7 and up. You will enjoy three daily fabulous meals and we do have a child friendly menu if needed. There are fun activities and hikes to enjoy. At night you will stay in a comfortable tent with sleeping bags and pillows. Don't worry about sitting on the ground because chairs are provided. We also provide two water-resistant bags to protect your personal belongings on the river. The starting cost of this trip is $1175 per adult and $975 per child.

Main Salmon 5 Day Rafting

This amazing experience beings in Salmon, Idaho and ends at McCall, Idaho for a total of 82 miles. This river is known as the "River of No Return", but don't worry you will return. The rapids along this section of the river range from class II to IV. This minimum age of this trip is 12 years. You will enjoy hiking along the river on this trip. Three mails a day are included. When sleeping you are provided with a tent, sleeping bag, and pillow. A camping chair is also provided along with a water-resistant bag to protect your personal belongings. The starting cost for this trip is $995 per person.

Snake River 5 Day Rafting

This experience begins in Cambridge, Idaho and ends in Lewiston, Idaho for a total of 79 miles. You will experience class II to IV rapids. You will travel through Hells Canyon which is the deepest river gorge in North America. The minimum age for this trip is 12 years. You will be provided with three delicious meals a day. We will also guide you through some easy and difficult hikes. Do not worry about sleeping equipment, we provide you with a tent, sleeping bag, and pillow. A chair is also included on this trip and a water resistant bag to protect your belongings. The starting cost of this trip is $1275 per person.
